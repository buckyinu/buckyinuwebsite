    What wallet should I use to join the fairlaunch?

    You can use a normal EVM dapp wallet like metamask or trust wallet.

    What will be the hard cap of this fairlaunch?

    Since its fair launch, there is no hard cap. But we are trying to get at least 25 BNB to start with a healthy LP. The tokens available for fairlaunch account for 45% of the total supply minted on the BSC chain. The number of tokens sold during our fairlaunch will determine the initial circulating supply of our tokens as well as the tokens minted on other chains when we go multichain.

    What slippage should we use on pancakeswap?

    Usually, tokens during the launch day tend to have higher slippage since the volume is still high and the price of the token fluctuates a lot.  As a result, I recommend 7-12% slippage during the launch and 1-5% after that.
